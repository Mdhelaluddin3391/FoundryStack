Case Studies

These are representative scenarios based on real startup problems. They demonstrate how we think and design systems — not marketing fiction.

Grocery Delivery MVP

Problem: Non-technical founder needed fast launch without locking into Shopify-style constraints.

Approach: Modular backend with inventory, order lifecycle, and delivery slot logic.

Outcome: MVP ready for real users and future mobile apps without rewrite.

Expert Booking Platform

Problem: Scheduling conflicts and unclear role permissions in early prototype.

Approach: Clean role-based access and booking rules separated from UI logic.

Outcome: Stable system ready for multiple expert categories.

Internal SaaS Dashboard

Problem: Company needed internal tooling that could later become a product.

Approach: Multi-tenant architecture from day one.

Outcome: Internal use first, external SaaS later — same codebase.
