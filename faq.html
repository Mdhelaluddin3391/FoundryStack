FAQ

Questions founders usually ask.

Clear answers, no vague promises. If something isn’t listed here, you can always reach out directly.

Do I really get full code ownership?

Yes. You receive the full Git repository, deployment files, and documentation. There is no lock-in — you’re free to hire in-house or move to another team anytime.

Do you use no-code tools like Bubble or Shopify?

No. I only use real code, primarily Django, with a modular monolith architecture. This keeps your system flexible and future-proof.

How long does a typical MVP take?

Most MVPs ship in 4–8 weeks, depending on scope. The first step is always architecture and feature prioritisation.

Can this scale later, or will we need a rewrite?

The architecture is designed to scale. You start with a clean modular monolith, which can later evolve into microservices without rewriting everything.

Can you act as a part-time CTO?

Yes. After launch, I can stay on as a fractional CTO — helping with code reviews, architecture decisions, and hiring guidance.

What if my idea doesn’t fit your existing patterns?

Then we design from scratch. Pre-built systems are there to save time, not to force your product into the wrong shape.

Still have questions?

Send a short note about your idea and I’ll answer honestly — even if it means suggesting a different approach.
