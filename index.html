The Problem

Most MVPs are built to be thrown away.

Founders often rush to build, only to realize months later that their code is a "black box" that hinders growth.

The "Speed Trap"

Agencies promising "2-week launches" often cut corners on security and database structure, leading to costly rewrites.

Vendor Lock-in

Reliance on proprietary no-code tools or obscure frameworks makes it nearly impossible to hire your own engineering team later.

Unstable Foundations

A prototype that works for 10 users often crashes at 100. We build systems designed for the 100 to 100,000 journey.
